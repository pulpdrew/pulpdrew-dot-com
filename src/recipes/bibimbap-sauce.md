### Ingredients:

- 60g gochujang
- 25g honey or sugar
- 5 g sesame oil
- 25 g rice vinegar
- ~1 Tbsp garlic, minced
- ~1 Tbsp Fresh Ginger, minced

### Directions:

1. Mix everything together
2. Add water as necessary to get the desired consistency
